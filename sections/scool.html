Project Overview

Just another day in the life of a middle school student. EXCEPT FOR THE FACT THAT THOSE BULLIES STOLE YOUR APPLE JUICE! Time to get back at them with the incredible powers provided to you by these miraculously cool sunglasses!

6 glasses, 6 different abilities to pick and choose from. Defeat wave after wave of unending bully hordes! Punch, dash, and "cool" your way to victory!

Key Features

Designing Around The Theme

With the theme of StreetFighter with the item reqired to be implemented being glasses we wanted to make the game in the most creative light possible.

Sound Effects & Music

We needed to utilize Sound Effects and Music to be the main source of feedback for the player. The music was heavily inspired by arcade game music with a touch of Rock and Roll.
